SMILE MAKEOVER

As every patient is different, we collaborate with our clients to create a smile that is unique and modelled to suit each individual case.
You are an excellent candidate for a Smile Makeover if you feel self-conscious about any of the following reasons:

Worn teeth
Discoloured teeth
Crooked teeth
Chipped teeth
Spaced or missing teeth
The process starts with a cosmetic consultation with the preferred dentist. We have a qualified team of specialists, nurses and staff to help you achieve the best results all under one roof.

THE PROCESS OF CREATING YOUR SMILE
The first step will be an initial assessment with your preferred cosmetic dentist at our clinic.

We will discuss your concerns and what is your ultima goal. A thorough examination will be carried out which includes general dental concerns, facial proportions, shape, colour, size and alignment of your teeth. This way we make sure to offer you the best recommendations and all different options available to you.

We can provide you with Computer Imaging Technology if you so wish. Which gives you an idea of how your smile would look through a 3D computer-aided smile design.

Once you have studied all the different options and have decided we would start planning your case, this includes taking impressions of your teeth to create a temporary template, which we can placed on top of your teeth which gives a better idea of how the final result would look like.

Appointments will then be made to carry out the treatment plan including the preparation, temporaries and fitting of the final custom made restorations. Review appointments are an important part of this process together with hygiene sessions to show you how best to care for your new beautiful smile.

Alongside ensuring the best result is created we also focus on your care and comfort throughout so the process of creating the smile of your dreams is as smooth for both yourself and us.
